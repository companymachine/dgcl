# Section 361

Law applicable to public benefit corporations; how formed.

- This subchapter applies to all public benefit corporations, as defined in ยง 362 of this title. If a corporation elects to become a public benefit corporation under this subchapter in the manner prescribed in this subchapter, it shall be subject in all respects to the provisions of this chapter, except to the extent this subchapter imposes additional or different requirements, in which case such requirements shall apply.
